Here's a few details about Marylin's life, off the screen:

  • Real Name: Norma Jeane Mortenson
  • Her First Appearance in Playboy: Was as much a surprise to her as it was to anyone who picked up that first issue of the magazine
  • Reason For Taking the Pictures: To pay for her mother to receive the best treatment money could buy for mental illness at the Betty Ford Clinic
